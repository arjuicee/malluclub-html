Malayalam Discord server

Want to talk Malayalam online without awkward group chats? Mallu Club is a social Discord hangout built around voice chat and community.

Malayalam community on Discord

Mallu Club started as a Kerala hangout and grew into one of the larger Malayalam Discord communities — about 2.3K members. People join to speak Malayalam, meet other Malayalis, and have a chill place online that isn't WhatsApp or Instagram DMs.

Voice chat in Malayalam

Voice is the main draw. Channels run in Malayalam, English, or both — whatever the conversation needs. Evenings are busiest. You can listen first, stay muted, or jump in when ready. See our voice chat page for more.

Not your typical server

Many Malayalam Discord servers are either dead or hyper-focused on one game. Mallu Club is a general social hangout. Talk about movies, Gulf life, Kerala news, or nothing in particular. The point is company, not a agenda.

Free and open

Discord is free. Mallu Club is free. Create an account, click the invite, accept the rules, and you're in. Full join guide here.
