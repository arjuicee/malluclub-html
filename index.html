FAQ

What is Mallu Club?

Mallu Club is a chill Kerala Discord hangout for Malayalis — about 2.4K members who come for voice chat, casual conversations, and community. Free to join.

How do I join the Kerala Discord server?

Click the Discord invite link, create a free Discord account if you need one, and accept the server rules. Works on phone, desktop, or browser.

Is Mallu Club free?

Yes. Joining and participating is completely free. Discord itself is free — you only need an account.

Why choose Mallu Club over other Malayalam Discord servers?

Most Malayali Discord servers go quiet or feel stiff. Mallu Club stays social — voice chat is usually going, people are friendly, and it's a relaxed place to hang out.

Who is Mallu Club for?

Malayalis anywhere who want a chill place to hang out — voice chat, casual Malayalam or English conversation, and a social community. Kerala, Gulf, abroad — all welcome.
