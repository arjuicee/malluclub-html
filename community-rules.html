Community rules

Mallu Club is a chill hangout. These rules keep it that way. Full rules are also posted inside Discord — this is the summary.

  • Be respectful. No harassment, hate speech, slurs, or targeted bullying. Disagree without being a jerk.
  • Keep it appropriate. No NSFW content, gore, or illegal stuff. Discord's terms apply too.
  • No spam. Don't flood channels, plug unrelated servers, or DM people unsolicited ads.
  • No drama farming. Don't stir up fights, expose private conversations, or bring offline beef into the server.
  • Voice chat etiquette. Don't scream, play loud music without consent, or hog the channel. Mute if you have background noise.
  • Listen to moderators. Mods keep the vibe chill. If asked to stop something, stop. Appeal calmly if you think it was a mistake.
  • Protect privacy. Don't share others' personal info without permission. Be careful what you share about yourself too.
  • Have fun. It's a hangout. Help keep it comfortable for new people and regulars alike.

Breaking rules can mean a warning, mute, kick, or ban depending on severity. Moderators have final say inside the server. The latest rules always live in Discord — check the rules channel after you join.
