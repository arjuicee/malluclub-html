How to join Mallu Club

Takes a few minutes. You need a free Discord account — that's it. No payment, no application form.

Official invite link: discord.gg/pnkGM8q9VA

  1. Open the invite link — click the button below or paste the link in your browser.
  2. Sign in to Discord — use an existing account or create one free at discord.com. Email or phone works.
  3. Accept the invite — Discord will ask you to join Mallu Club. Click Accept Invite.
  4. Check the rules — read the rules channel so you know what's expected. Pick any starter roles if the server asks.
  5. Hang out — browse text channels or join a voice channel when you feel like it. Lurking is totally fine.

Join on mobile

Install the Discord app from the App Store or Google Play, create an account, then open the invite link on your phone. It should open directly in the app.

Join on desktop

Use the Discord desktop app or discord.com in your browser. Same invite link works everywhere.

Is it really free?

Yes. Discord is free. Mallu Club has no membership fee. You will never be asked to pay to join or hang out.
