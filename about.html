About Mallu Club

A chill Discord hangout for Malayalis — not a grind server, not a dead server. Just voice chat, social community, and casual talk.

What we are

Mallu Club is a Kerala Malayalam Discord community with about 2.4K members. Most are from Kerala or the Gulf, but people join from Europe, the US, and anywhere Malayalis live. The vibe is social — hop into voice, chat in text, hang out. No pressure.

What we're not

We're not a competitive gaming clan, a study-only server, or a formal organization. You don't need to introduce yourself with a resume. Lurking is fine. Jumping into voice when you feel like it is fine too.

Voice chat & community

Voice channels are the heart of Mallu Club. Evenings (IST and Gulf time) are usually the busiest — people talking Malayalam, English, or a mix. Text channels run alongside for memes, random chat, and links. Moderators keep spam and drama out so it stays comfortable.

Who runs it

Mallu Club is community-run on Discord. This website (malludiscord.shop) is the official landing page with the invite link and info. For questions or issues, reach out inside the server or on our social channels.
