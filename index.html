FAQ

What is Mallu Club?

Mallu Club is a Kerala Discord server for Malayalis. It brings together 2.3K+ members for Malayalam chat, gaming, study groups, and community events — online and free.

How do I join the Kerala Discord server?

Click the Discord invite link, create a free Discord account if you need one, and accept the server rules. Works on phone, desktop, or browser.

Is Mallu Club free?

Yes. Joining and participating is completely free. Discord itself is free — you only need an account.

Why choose Mallu Club over other Malayalam Discord servers?

Most Malayali Discord servers go quiet after the first week. Mallu Club stays active with daily voice chat, regular events, custom bots, and active moderation to keep spam out.

Who is Mallu Club for?

Malayalis in Kerala, the Gulf, Europe, or anywhere else who want gaming teammates, study partners, or casual Malayalam conversation. Students, gamers, and NRIs all welcome.
