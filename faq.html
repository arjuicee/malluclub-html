FAQ

Common questions about Mallu Club — the Kerala Malayalam Discord hangout.

What is Mallu Club?

Mallu Club is a chill Kerala Discord hangout for Malayalis — about 2.3K members who come for voice chat, casual conversations, and community. Free to join.

How do I join?

Click discord.gg/pnkGM8q9VA, sign in or create a free Discord account, accept the invite. See our step-by-step guide for details.

Is Mallu Club free?

Yes. Discord is free and Mallu Club has no membership fee. You will never be asked to pay to join.

Do I have to use voice chat?

No. Many people hang out in text channels only. Voice is there when you want it — listen muted, say hi when ready, or skip it entirely.

What language do people speak?

Mostly Malayalam and English, often mixed. Whatever you're comfortable with works.

Who is Mallu Club for?

Malayalis anywhere who want a chill place to hang out — Kerala, Gulf, Europe, US, anywhere. Social community, not age-restricted but keep it appropriate.

Why Mallu Club over other Malayalam Discord servers?

Many Malayali servers go quiet or feel stiff. Mallu Club stays social with active voice chat and a relaxed vibe. Moderators keep spam out.

Is this a gaming or study server?

Not primarily. It's a social hangout. People might mention games or exams in conversation, but the main point is community and voice chat.

How do I contact moderators?

Inside the Discord server — look for mod channels or DM a moderator. You can also reach us on Instagram or Twitter.
