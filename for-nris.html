For NRIs & Gulf Malayalis

Living abroad and missing casual Malayalam conversation? Mallu Club is a chill Discord hangout where Malayalis from Dubai, Saudi, Europe, and everywhere else come to talk.

Home is far, but the hangout isn't

When you're in the Gulf or anywhere outside Kerala, finding people to just talk Malayalam with — not formal groups, not WhatsApp forwards — can be hard. Mallu Club is that casual space. Voice chat at night after work, text channels during the day, people who get the NRI/Gulf experience.

Time zones actually work

Evening in Dubai overlaps with night in Kerala. That means voice channels often have a mix of Gulf residents and people back home — perfect for random conversations, movie talk, or complaining about traffic in two countries at once.

What people talk about

  • Life abroad — work, visa stuff, missing home food
  • Malayalam movies, music, and memes
  • Kerala news and festival nostalgia
  • Random hangout talk — same as any friend group

No pressure to be "active"

Join, lurk, pop into voice once a week or every night — up to you. About 2.3K members means there's usually someone around without it feeling overwhelming.
