Kerala Discord server

Mallu Club is one of the more active Kerala-focused Discord communities — a chill hangout with voice chat, not a dead link from 2021.

Why Mallu Club?

Plenty of "Kerala Discord server" invites go quiet after a week. Mallu Club has stuck around with 2.3K+ members, active voice chat, and moderators who actually show up. It's a social hangout — Malayalis talking, joking, hanging out — not a formal club with applications.

What you get

  • Malayalam and English voice & text chat
  • Members from Kerala, Gulf, and abroad
  • Chill community vibe — no grind required
  • Free on Discord, join in minutes

Who joins

Students, working professionals, NRIs, people back in Kochi or Calicut — anyone Malayali who wants an online hangout. You don't need to be hardcore into gaming or anything specific. Show up, talk if you want, listen if you don't.
